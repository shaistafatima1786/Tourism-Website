Destinations of Nepal

Temple

Explore the rich cultural and spiritual heritage of Nepal by visiting its ancient temples.From the majestic Pashupatinath Temple on the banks of the Bagmati River to the intricately carved temples in Kathmandu Durbar Square, each site tells a story of devotion and architectural marvel.

Mustang

Embark on a journey to Mustang, often referred to as the "Last Forbidden Kingdom." .Experience Tibetan culture, witness the stunning views of Nilgiri and Dhaulagiri peaks, and traverse the Kali Gandaki Gorge, making Mustang a hidden gem for adventurous souls.

Begnas Tal

Nestled in the serene Pokhara Valley, Begnas Tal offers a tranquil escape for nature lovers. Surrounded by lush greenery and framed by the Annapurna Mountain Range, this pristine lake provides a perfect setting for boating and relaxation.

Boudhanath Stupa

Boudhanath Stupa, one of the largest stupas in the world, stands as a symbol of peace and enlightenment. Located in the heart of Kathmandu, it's a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a sacred pilgrimage site for Buddhists,devotees circumambulate this iconic structure.
